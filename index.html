Portfolio

Below, you will find some examples of my work in data visualization, R programming, GIS analysis and more.

  • Interactive visualizations of FAO’s fisheries and aquaculture data
    • Two versions: International trade data / Global production data
    • Technology: R Shiny, Highcharts
    • Data: FAO
    • To be featured on FAO’s website soon
    • GitHub source available

  • Animated maps of global fisheries and aquaculture production by country
    • Technology: R (ggplot2, sf, gganimate packages)
    • Data: FAO
    • Featured on FAO’s Fisheries and Aquaculture Division’s Twitter/X account
    • GitHub source available

    • Technology: Flourish
    • Data: FAO
    • Featured in ICES ASC 2019 (keynote presentation), FAO’s International Symposium on Fisheries Sustainability (keynote presentation) and on FAO’s fisheries and aquaculture statistics website

  • Map showing the results of a study of climate change’s effects on fisheries sustainability
    • Technology: QGIS
    • Data: FAO
    • Presented at COP25

  • GIS analysis of the urban heat island effect in Brussels based on multispectral satellite imagery
    • Technology: ArcGIS
    • Data: LandSat 8
